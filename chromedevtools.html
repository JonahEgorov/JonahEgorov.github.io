Chrome DevTools

This is a cool video I found by searching "How to use Chrome Dev Tools" https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wcFnnxfA70gI

I Really didn't know anything about Chrome Dev Tools. I only stumbled across them once accidentally before and never really thought about them. I didn't realize that you can use them to alter the page and do all this complex stuff. However what amazed me the most was being able to see how HTML works in the real world and also how HTML and CSS work together. One thing that I thought might be useful is if you were having a problem creating your own site, you could just go to another site and see how they made it work. 

I thought the video was at a pretty basic level and I was able to understand him very well. The coolest thing I learned about is being able to see what it looks like on a mobile phone, very useful especially in this age of web development.
