SEO for eCommerce Part 1

Understanding SEO

It’s the process of assessing and improving factors to increase rankings in the search engines. It’s an ongoing process of making improvements and constantly building upon them. YOu must focus on building traffic to build rankings. Focus on building Traffic. ON-Page is stuff done on the page. Off-Page is stuff like social media you do off your page. Make sure the site is easy to use for customers. Acquisition and Retention. Make thing easy for customers to find. Focus on Proper Design Techniques. You can move up and down the funnel depending on what you need to reach. It is an ongoing process. Focus on more specific targets.

Researching Your Customers

You can get feedback with surveys, to find out more about your customers. Identify themes in customers. Group them into personas and make a group of people or make a purchase. Find out exactly who is buying your products. Customer age, gender, location, job titles, and interest they may have. You can optimize the site to that persona. Quality Traffic = Higher Sales. Simple Keywords are usually one to two words. Long tail keywords are generally three or more words targeting something very specific. You need both. Use google Adwords. Quality is more important than Quantity. Analyze why your competitors are at the top. Side by side SEO comparison is pretty good tool for doing a side by side comparison.

Auditing for Site Usability and Structure

Page Title is one of the most important. It should contain most popular keywords. Make sure page titles are 50-60 characters long. Use most critical keywords ideally close to beginning of the tag. You can get a preview on Shopify. You can also click edit shop SEO. YOu want people to understand what the page is about on the page title and URL. Page desc META has a maximum character length. Make sure prominent heading texts uses the pro[per heading tags. Make sure product descriptions uses key words. Use description for image. Don’t forget about Chrome Dev Tools when designing the website for SEO. You want to use good links and lots of them to link around your website. Large footer areas are good for several navigation links. Building links from external websites is crucial to building your website into your own site. Getting a link into your site from one of these top sites is SEO gold. Learn to build quality inbound links.
