Web Development or Web Related Jobs

I searched "Web Development Jobs from Home" using google search engine and the first site it took me to was indeed for "Front End Web Developer's Work From Home Jobs". I thought it would be interesting to know what the work from some jobs where in this type of field.

Here are some of the jobs I found:

Full Stack Web Application Developer 
AdvantageData - Boston, MA 02110
$70,000 - $150,000 a year

About the Position:
As a member of a highly skilled and growing development team, candidate will be involved in front end development of Advantage Data’s web-based Wall Street information system, which is used by investment professionals all around the world. Candidate will use HTML5, JavaScript, React, SQL, and ColdFusion, CSS and Linux. Good UI or graphic design skills are a plus. Other skills which are helpful but not required include: AngularJS, Polymer, Ember, Backbone, NodeJS, Flex and/or Bootstrap. Be at the forefront of high finance and technology. Learn all about Wall Street. Family-friendly work schedule, beautiful office with spectacular views of Boston, fully stocked kitchen and many other benefits.

Location:
Downtown Boston. Easy commute, within 5 minutes walk of North Station, South Station and Park Street Station.

About Advantage Data:
Advantage Data is a dynamic, rapidly growing, solidly profitable, Inc 500 Wall Street information provider located in Boston, New York City and London, specializing in corporate bonds, syndicated bank loans, credit derivatives and equities. Advantage Data services hundreds of Wall Street firms such as Bank of America Merrill Lynch, Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, Credit Suisse, Citigroup, Fidelity, etc. in 15 countries worldwide (United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Ireland, Germany, Finland, Italy, Portugal, Saudi Arabia, China, Japan, Netherlands, Norway, South Africa, Sweden, Hong Kong, Singapore, etc.). Advantage Data's patented technology is used in the $55 trillion bond and credit derivatives over-the-counter securities markets worldwide.

Compensation:
$70K-$150K, healthcare, dental, commuter benefits, 401K, short and long-term disability, life insurance, 15 vacation/sick days, 9 paid holidays, 3-9 work-at-home days, employee referral bonus program
