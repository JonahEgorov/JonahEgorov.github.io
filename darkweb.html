Dark Web

This is a very delicate subject. Privacy vs Stopping Illegal Activity. I didn't know anything about the Dark Web before watching this video. I am glad that I was able to learn more about the Dark Web. I believe we should all have a great deal of privacy. Privacy is very important. However I think selling and buying illegal items is very bad. The question becomes shall we sacrifice our freedom and privacy to stop illegal activity. What usually happens is the law abiding people will stop using it if it became illegal however all the unlawful people will continue to use it for bad things. 

Let's take the example of a gun. A good person can have a gun and use it only for good intentions such as self defense, a bad person can also have a gun and use it for unlawful things. They both have the same tool. Most great things that are made have bad uses, to some people the regular internet can be used for horrible things. Let's take a phone, the great technological advance of the phone has increased communication for all the good people, an example is police can respond quicker but bad people can also use the fast communication. I think with every stride towards something greater, something bad will also come. Hopefully we find ways to keep things encrypted and private and stop illegal activity.
